Welcome to our Minecraft crafting recipe box! Here you will find every recipe that can be crafted by using either the Minecraft crafting table or the crafting area that is available within your personal inventory. We have recipes that you can use to craft your very first basic items upon entering Minecraft and also recipes for crafting weapons, armor, food, tools, blocks and much more! We highly suggest that you carve out a home base for yourself and create a crafting table as soon as possible!
